Overview

My research investigates heavy-tail emergence in stochastic gradient methods (SGD with momentum, Decentralized SGD), their dynamics, and generalization properties. I also work on quantitative finance applications including derivatives pricing, interest rate modeling, and risk measurement, bridging theory and robust computational practice.

Advisor: Dr. Lingjiong Zhu

Areas & Current Topics

  • Heavy-tailed Stochastic Optimization; Pearson diffusions & tail indices
  • SGD Dynamics & Generalization; algorithms with momentum and decentralization
  • Monte Carlo methods; variance reduction; Greeks and sensitivities
  • Interest rate modeling; credit risk; risk management
  • Deep learning for financial prediction
